Data Analyst Intern

Preferred Skills:

- Familiarity with cloud data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift)
- Experience with version control (Git)

Minimum Qualification: Pursuing or completed B.Tech/B.E. in Computer Science,
Data Science, or related field.
